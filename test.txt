MEDICAL EXPERT SYSTEM - TEST SCENARIOS
All 20 diseases across all 5 groups.

================================================================================
GROUP A - RESPIRATORY
================================================================================

Test 1 - Influenza
Patient statement: "I have a fever and my whole body is aching"
Confirm yes
Answer YES to: cough, fatigue, headache, chills, sudden onset
Answer NO to: diarrhea, neck stiffness
Expected Result: Influenza ~100%

---

Test 2 - Common Cold
Patient statement: "I have a runny nose and keep sneezing"
Confirm yes
Answer YES to: cough, sore throat, fatigue
Answer NO to: fever, body aches, chills
Expected Result: Common Cold ~100%

---

Test 3 - Pneumonia
Patient statement: "I have a fever and I keep coughing up phlegm"
Confirm yes
Answer YES to: fatigue, chest pain, difficulty breathing
Answer NO to: diarrhea, neck stiffness
Expected Result: Pneumonia ~100%

---

Test 4 - Tuberculosis
Patient statement: "I have had a persistent cough for weeks and I keep waking up drenched in sweat"
Confirm yes
Answer YES to: weight loss, fatigue, low grade fever, chest pain
Answer NO to: sudden onset, diarrhea
Expected Result: Tuberculosis ~87%

NOTE: TB does not reach 100% unless haemoptysis is also confirmed - that is intentional and clinically correct.

================================================================================
EDGE CASE TESTS
================================================================================

Edge Case 1 - Migraine vs Meningitis (critical differentiator)
Patient statement: "I have a severe headache and sensitivity to light"
Confirm yes
Answer YES to: neck stiffness, fever, confusion
Answer NO to: pulsating pain, one sided pain, visual aura
Expected Result: Meningitis (neck stiffness + fever eliminates migraine via hard rules)

---

Edge Case 2 - Tension Headache vs Migraine
Patient statement: "I have a headache and feel tired"
Confirm yes
Answer YES to: dizziness, light sensitivity, neck stiffness
Answer NO to: fever, vomiting, pulsating pain, one sided pain, visual aura
Expected Result: Tension Headache (absence of aura and pulsating pain is the key split)

---

Edge Case 3 - Gastroenteritis vs Appendicitis (RLQ pain)
Patient statement: "I have stomach pain and feel nauseous"
Confirm yes
Answer YES to: right lower quad pain, rebound tenderness, fever
Answer NO to: diarrhea
Expected Result: Appendicitis (diarrhea NO + RLQ pain eliminates gastroenteritis)

---

Edge Case 4 - TB vs Pneumonia (chronicity)
Patient statement: "I have been coughing for weeks and I am losing weight"
Confirm yes
Answer YES to: night sweats, fatigue, low grade fever, chest pain
Answer NO to: sudden onset, diarrhea
Expected Result: Tuberculosis (chronic_cough + night_sweats + weight_loss distinguishes from pneumonia)

---

Edge Case 5 - Inconclusive (contradictory profile)
Patient statement: "I have a fever and my hair is falling out"
Confirm yes
Answer NO to everything else asked
Expected Result: Inconclusive (fever eliminates hypothyroidism; too few symptoms for any other disease)
