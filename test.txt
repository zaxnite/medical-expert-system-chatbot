EDGE CASES
============================================================

Edge 1 - Inconclusive (contradictory profile)
----------------------------------------
Free text: I have a fever and I keep gaining weight
Symptoms detected: fever, weight_gain

Symptoms to say YES to:
  - I feel tired and weak            [fatigue]
  - I have a headache                [headache]

Symptoms to say NO to:
  - everything else

Expected: Inconclusive
Reason: fever hard-rules out all chronic diseases (hypothyroidism,
        diabetes, anemia etc.), weight_gain hard-rules out all acute
        infectious diseases. No disease survives both constraints.
